Lion

King of Jungle

The lion, often known as the "King of the Jungle", is one of the most powerful and iconic big cats on Earth. Lions live in family groups called prides, consisting of females, cubs, and a few males. Male lions are distinguished by their large manes, while lionesses do most of the hunting. Lions are apex predators and play a crucial role in maintaining ecosystem balance. They are found mainly in sub-Saharan Africa, with a small population of Asiatic lions living in the Gir Forest of India.
